Growing up, Transformers was one of my favorite cartoons and movie franchises. What drew me in was the combination of action, unique characters, and the idea that ordinary vehicles could transform into powerful robots with their own personalities and stories. Each Autobot and Decepticon had different strengths, motivations, and leadership styles, making every battle exciting and memorable.
